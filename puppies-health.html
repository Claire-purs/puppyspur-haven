Your Puppy’s Health is Everything

We’re dedicated to ensuring your new companion arrives healthy, happy, and ready for cuddles. Our health protocols are designed to give you complete peace of mind.

Every puppy receives a nose-to-tail health check before coming home.

Our puppies are raised in loving home environments with the highest standards of care. Before leaving us, every puppy undergoes a comprehensive veterinary examination to ensure they are in perfect health.

  • Full physical exam covering eyes, ears, mouth, heart, lungs, and more
  • Age-appropriate vaccinations and deworming
  • Microchip pre-registered to you
  • Health certificate for safe travel

Our Comprehensive Health Evaluation

Every Puppy Pur Corgi receives thorough veterinary screening by a licensed veterinarian. This “nose-to-tail” exam checks for any signs of illness or congenital issues, ensuring your puppy is 100% ready for their new home.

What’s Included:

  • Complete physical examination
  • Fecal test for parasites
  • Up-to-date vaccinations & deworming
  • Microchip implantation & registration
  • Official health certificate

2-Year Health Guarantee

We stand behind the health of every Puppy Pur Corgi with our industry-leading 2-year health guarantee against life-threatening genetic conditions.

Covered Conditions Include:

  • Degenerative Myelopathy (DM)
  • von Willebrand’s Disease (vWD1)
  • Exercise-Induced Collapse (EIC)
  • Other serious congenital defects

If diagnosed by a licensed veterinarian within 2 years, we will replace your puppy with one of equal value when available.

*Requires vet exam within 72 hours of arrival and proof of continued care. Full terms available upon request.
