Your Puppy’s Health is Everything

We’re dedicated to ensuring your new companion arrives healthy, happy, and ready for a lifetime of love. Our rigorous health protocols give you complete peace of mind.

Nose-to-Tail Health Check

Every Puppy Pur Corgi receives a thorough veterinary examination before going home — ensuring they are in perfect health and ready for their new family.

  • Full physical exam (eyes, ears, heart, lungs, joints & more)
  • Age-appropriate vaccinations & multiple dewormings
  • Fecal parasite screening
  • Pre-registered microchip
  • Official health certificate

Premium Veterinary Care

Our puppies are raised with love and the highest standards. Each one sees a licensed veterinarian for a complete “nose-to-tail” evaluation — so you can welcome them home with total confidence.

Every Puppy Receives:

  • Comprehensive physical examination
  • Up-to-date vaccinations & deworming
  • Microchip implantation & registration
  • Parasite screening
  • Travel-ready health certificate

2-Year Genetic Health Guarantee

We proudly stand behind every Puppy Pur Corgi with an industry-leading 2-year guarantee against life-threatening genetic conditions.

Covered Conditions Include:

  • Degenerative Myelopathy (DM)
  • von Willebrand’s Disease (vWD)
  • Exercise-Induced Collapse (EIC)
  • Other serious congenital defects

If diagnosed within 2 years, we provide a replacement puppy of equal value from a future litter.

*Requires initial vet exam within 72 hours & proof of ongoing care. Full terms provided at purchase.
