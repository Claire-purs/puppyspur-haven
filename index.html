About Puppy Pur

Family-owned in Hillsboro, Texas (USDA #74-A-2468), we’ve been raising happy, healthy Pembroke Welsh Corgis and rare Merle Corgis since 2014. Every puppy is born and raised in our home — never cages, never kennels. Parents are AKC-registered, OFA health-tested, and DNA-clear. We offer a full 2-year health guarantee, microchip, up-to-date shots, deworming, and lifetime breeder support. Over 91+ happy families nationwide have welcomed a Puppy Pur Corgi into their lives. We ship safely coast-to-coast with a nanny or drive delivery available. Your perfect short-legged best friend is waiting!
