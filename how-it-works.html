1

Find Your Perfect Puppy

Browse our hand-selected Pembroke Welsh and rare Merle Corgis. View photos, videos, parents, and full details. Chat with us for extra pictures or video calls — we’ll help you find the one that matches your family’s energy and lifestyle.

2

Reserve & Secure

Once you’ve found “the one,” place a $500 deposit via secure payment. Your puppy is held exclusively for you. We accept PayPal, Venmo, Cash App, and Zelle — fast and safe.

3

Health & Readiness Check

Every puppy gets age-appropriate vaccines, deworming (3x), microchip (pre-registered to you), and a full vet exam with health certificate. We only release puppies when they’re 100% ready.

4

Delivery Coordination & Arrival

We arrange safe nationwide travel — flight with nanny or personal drive delivery. You get tracking every step of the way. Your puppy arrives with starter pack: food, toy, and blanket with mom’s scent.

5

Lifetime Support

Welcome home! You get our 2-year health guarantee, lifetime breeder support, and a family that’s always here for questions, advice, or just to see how your Corgi is doing.
