Terms of Sale

Clear, fair, and transparent policies designed to protect both you and your new Corgi companion.

General Terms

  • All sales are final — no cash refunds will be issued.
  • A $500 non-refundable deposit secures your selected puppy and position in line.
  • Remaining balance is due in full before delivery or pickup.
  • Accepted payment methods: PayPal • Venmo • Cash App • Zelle
  • Puppies are released only when age-appropriate, fully vetted, and healthy.

Delivery & Shipping

  • Nationwide ground or flight nanny delivery available.
  • All delivery/shipping costs and arrangements are the buyer’s responsibility.
  • Puppies travel with health certificate, vaccination records, microchip, and starter pack.
  • Buyer must be present to receive the puppy or arrange an approved representative.

Buyer Responsibilities

  • Schedule a full veterinary exam within 72 hours of arrival and provide us a copy of the report.
  • Provide lifelong proper care, nutrition, socialization, and routine veterinary visits.
  • Follow recommended vaccination schedule until fully protected (16–20 weeks).
  • Avoid high-risk public areas (dog parks, pet stores) until vaccinations are complete.
  • Notify us immediately of any health or welfare concerns.

Failure to meet these responsibilities voids all health guarantees.

Registration & Breeding Rights

  • Pet-quality puppies include limited AKC registration (no breeding rights).
  • Full breeding rights available for approved homes at additional cost.
  • All registered names must include “PuppysPur” prefix or suffix.
  • We reserve the right to refuse sale to any buyer for any reason.

Additional Policies

  • No guarantee on final adult size, coat type/length, ear set, bite alignment, or exact temperament (influenced by environment).
  • We do not guarantee against treatable conditions (parasites, giardia, kennel cough, etc.).
  • PuppysPur reserves the right to reclaim any puppy found in neglectful conditions.
  • Placing a deposit indicates full acceptance of these terms.
