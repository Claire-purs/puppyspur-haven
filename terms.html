PuppysPur Terms of Sales

Transparent and fair policies to protect you and your new Corgi companion.

General Terms

  • All sales are final. No cash refunds will be issued.
  • A $500 non-refundable deposit secures your chosen puppy.
  • Full payment is required before delivery.
  • We accept PayPal, Venmo, Cash App, and Zelle.
  • Puppies are released only when age-appropriate and fully vetted.

Delivery & Shipping

  • Nationwide delivery available via flight nanny or personal drive.
  • All shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer.
  • Puppies travel with health certificate, starter pack, and all records.
  • Buyer must be present to receive puppy or arrange approved pickup.

Buyer Responsibilities

  • Schedule veterinary exam within 72 hours of arrival and send us a copy.
  • Provide proper care, nutrition, socialization, and routine veterinary visits.
  • Follow vaccination schedule until fully protected (16–20 weeks).
  • Avoid high-risk areas (dog parks, pet stores) until vaccinations complete.
  • Notify us immediately of any health concerns.

Failure to follow these responsibilities voids all guarantees.

Registration & Breeding Rights

  • Pet puppies come with limited AKC registration (no breeding rights).
  • Full registration available for approved breeding homes at additional cost.
  • All registered names must include “Puppy Pur” prefix or suffix.
  • We reserve the right to refuse sale to any buyer.

Additional Notes

  • No guarantee on final size, coat, ear set, bite, or temperament (influenced by environment).
  • We do not guarantee against treatable conditions (parasites, giardia, kennel cough, etc.).
  • Puppy Pur reserves the right to reclaim any puppy not properly cared for.
  • By placing a deposit, you agree to these terms.
