Puppy Pur Health Guarantee

Your new Corgi’s health and happiness are our highest priority. We stand behind every puppy with our comprehensive 2-year health guarantee.

2-Year Genetic Health Guarantee

To the best of our knowledge, every Puppy Pur Corgi is in excellent health at the time of purchase and free from visible signs of infection or contagious disease.

All puppies receive age-appropriate vaccinations (Parvo, Distemper, Adenovirus, Para Influenza) and deworming before going home.

If, within 2 years of purchase, a licensed veterinarian diagnoses your puppy with a life-threatening congenital or genetic condition (including DM, vWD1, EIC), we will, at our discretion:

  • Provide a replacement puppy of equal value when available, or
  • Reimburse reasonable veterinary expenses related to the condition (up to the purchase price of the puppy)

We reserve the right to require a second opinion from a veterinarian of our choice at our expense.

This guarantee is void if the diagnosis is inconclusive or related to environmental factors (diet, trauma, etc.).

Lifetime Rehoming Guarantee

We never want a Puppy Pur Corgi to end up in a shelter. If you are ever unable to care for your dog — at any stage of their life — contact us. We will help find them a loving new home or take them back ourselves.

Buyer Responsibilities & Conditions

  • Take your puppy to a licensed veterinarian for a full exam within 72 hours of arrival and send us a copy of the report.
  • Provide proper care: annual vet visits, vaccinations, quality diet, and safe environment.
  • Follow recommended vaccination schedule until at least 16–20 weeks of age.
  • Do not expose puppy to high-risk areas (dog parks, pet stores, etc.) until fully vaccinated.
  • Notify us immediately of any health concerns.

Failure to follow these conditions voids the guarantee. This guarantee applies only to the original buyer and is non-transferable.

What Is Not Covered

  • Common treatable conditions (parasites, giardia, kennel cough, etc.)
  • Conditions caused by injury, neglect, or improper care
  • Cosmetic traits (coat color, eye color, size, ear set, bite alignment)
  • Parvo (due to environmental exposure risk)

All sales are final. No cash refunds. Replacement puppy only when available.
