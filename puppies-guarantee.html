Our Health Guarantee

Your new family member’s health and happiness are our top priority. Every Puppy Pur Corgi comes with industry-leading protection and lifelong support.

2-Year Genetic Health Guarantee

Every Puppy Pur Corgi leaves in excellent health and free from known contagious or infectious diseases.

All puppies receive age-appropriate vaccinations and multiple dewormings before going home.

If a licensed veterinarian diagnoses a life-threatening congenital or genetic condition within 2 years of purchase (including DM, vWD, EIC), we will:

  • Provide a replacement puppy of equal value from a future litter, or
  • Reimburse reasonable veterinary expenses related to the condition (up to the original purchase price)

We reserve the right to request a second veterinary opinion at our expense.

This guarantee does not cover conditions caused by environment, injury, neglect, or improper care.

Lifetime Rehoming Support

No Puppy Pur dog will ever be homeless. If life circumstances change and you can no longer care for your corgi — at any age — contact us. We will personally help rehome them or welcome them back into our family.

Your Responsibilities

  • Schedule a full veterinary exam within 72 hours of arrival and share the report with us
  • Provide annual vet care, proper nutrition, safe environment, and continued vaccinations
  • Avoid high-risk areas (dog parks, pet stores) until fully vaccinated (16–20 weeks)
  • Notify us immediately of any health concerns

Failure to meet these requirements voids the health guarantee. Guarantee is non-transferable and applies only to the original purchaser.

What Is Not Covered

  • Treatable conditions (giardia, coccidia, worms, kennel cough, etc.)
  • Issues from injury, neglect, or improper care
  • Cosmetic or non-life-threatening traits (coat color changes, size variation, undescended testicles after 6 months, bite alignment)
  • Parvovirus (due to environmental exposure risk)

All sales are final • No cash refunds

Replacement puppy provided when available from future litters.
